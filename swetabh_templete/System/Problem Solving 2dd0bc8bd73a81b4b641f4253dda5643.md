### **Problem solving template (SHERLOCK HOLMES)**

- What am I doing wrong? (what is the wrong thing)
- What is the **trigger** that makes me do this?
- **IS THIS A PATTERN THAT I DO DAILY?**
- What do I need to change to avoid the trigger?
- What do I do once I start doing the wrong thing?
- **How is it harming my life in the long term?**

(computation) 

- **What should I be doing instead?**

(realization → capitalize) 

- **How would that benefit me?**

(incentive) 

---

- **What is the nature of the problem? (Emotional. Financial. House abuse. Spouse)**
- **What is the emotional impact of the problem in %?**

Ex: 50% f**ks me up emotionally.

- **Do I have a strategy to deal with the emotional disturbances it causes? (do I grieve appropriately? Do I meditate to relieve the stress? Do I do problem solving? Do I use cognitive exercises? Do I have a strategy? Do I have a list of actions to follow to minimize getting affected by it?)**
- Do I have the power to solve this problem?

(yes, it depends on me. No, it is coming from external source of authority) 

- **What do I have the power to do and change?**
- **How will that get me out of this in the long term?**

</aside>

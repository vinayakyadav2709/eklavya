# **If you don’t subtract and schedule, you will forget where time went. if you do, all your dreams will come true.**

**How to operate:** 

- Whenever you think about getting productive, for example, **you wake up**, think **how many hours do you have** before you have to do something (for ex: sleep, meet somebody, class)
- Let’s say you have 6 hours at hand, what are the important things you have to do right now.
- List them down.
- Let’s say 5 hours left. Divide the tasks, assign time, and start doing the first.
- For example, between class and evening meeting, **you have 4 hours, play with those 4 hours.**
- **Formula**: Hours left x important tasks to do x start doing = the best way to get shit done, phone disappears
- “I will work” — at what time? what precise task? for how long? — when this is absent, you are going to waste time

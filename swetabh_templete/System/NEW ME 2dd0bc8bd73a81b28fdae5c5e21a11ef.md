### **Read everyday or whenever in doubt:**

- Time will solve the emotions of your problems. 
You will waste a lot of your time if you believe you can solve things by just by problem solving. This will also result in frustration when results don’t come immediately. 
A lot of times, all you need is a conclusion: “I don’t need this”, or this will happen once this is achieved, or this will happen in 2 years. However, the brain will still make you think about it. That is when you have to become extremely busy. You cannot pick or choose to put it in the background, it will be placed there by actions toward choosing what needs to be on the foreground.
- If you have a solution or conclusion, stick with it. It may take 3 months to apply. Impatience to want to make it happen instantly will make it 3 years.
- Be a train that has set off to its destination. There is no point of stopping once you know what you know.
- One of the best ways to end overthinking is by staying busy. Decide what to do and do exactly that.
- One very important thing: your moods and certain ideas may go back like a pendulum. Allow it. Feeling something does not mean it is reveals some grand truths or realizations. “I love my ex therefore she was the one”. That’s a logical fallacy. You miss somebody, you feel bad about something, you are in a shitty mood for some reason. The truth is: a) it is temporary b) there is no grand revelation c) no need to overthink, continue sticking to your plan d) in time, it will stop completely.
